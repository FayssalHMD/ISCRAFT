Meet the Challenge: ISCraft 2025

Welcome to the inaugural ISCraft competition! Dive into the world of robotics by designing, building, and programming a functional robotic arm to tackle unique challenges. It's your platform to innovate, compete, and connect.

View Challenge Specs

Why Participate?

Sharpen Your Skills

Apply theory to practice. Design, build, and program a functional robotic arm, tackling real-world engineering challenges and honing your technical abilities.

Connect & Collaborate

Work alongside talented peers. Share ideas, learn from others, and build valuable connections within the robotics community in a stimulating team environment.

Learn Through Workshops

Benefit from expert-led workshops designed to provide guidance, enhance your skills, and help you tackle the challenges with confidence.

Frequently Asked Questions

The competition is open to university students and officially registered university scientific clubs in Algeria.

Each team must consist of 4 members or less.

The competition is divided into two phases:

Preliminary Round: Participants must submit a video showcasing their robotic arm in action.

The qualified teams will participate in online workshops about robotic arms and automation before competing on-site to complete the given challenges.
